Jupyter Notebooks for Retrieving NOAA National Water Model (NWM) Retrospective Data

This resource includes the following Jupyter Notebooks and associated Python files with functions called by the notebooks:
 - NWM_output_variable_retrieval_with_FeatureID.ipynb
 - NWM_output_variable_retrieval_with_shapefile.ipynb

The resource is designed for use on a JupyterHub platform connected to CUAHSI HydroShare and has been tested on CUAHSI JupyterHub, CyberGIS-Jupyter for Water, and CIROH JupyterHub.

To use this HydroShare resource, select "Open With..." on any of these application servers to launch the respective JupyterHub environment. Then, open the Jupyter Notebook of your choice and follow the provided instructions.
